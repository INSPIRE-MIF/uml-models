-- Definition --<br /></p><p>A conceptual geological feature that is hypothesized to exist coherently in the world.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Description --<br /></p><p>This corresponds with a "legend item" from a traditional geologic map. While the bounding coordinates of a Geologic Feature may be described, its shape is not.<br /></p><p>The implemented Geologic Feature instance acts as the "description package"<br /></p>
