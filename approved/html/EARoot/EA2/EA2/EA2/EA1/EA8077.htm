-- Definition --<br/>A conceptual geological feature that is hypothesized to exist coherently in the world.<br/><br/>-- Description --<br/>This corresponds with a "legend item" from a traditional geologic map. While the bounding coordinates of a Geologic Feature may be described, its shape is not.<br/>The implemented Geologic Feature instance acts as the "description package"<br/><br/>
