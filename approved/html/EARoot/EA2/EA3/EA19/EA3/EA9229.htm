-- Name --<br/>Vector statistical unit geometry<br/><br/>-- Definition --<br/>A geometrical representation for vector statistical units.<br/><br/>-- Description --<br/>Vector statistical units can have several representations depending on the context they are used for. It is adviced to produce at least one reference geometry.<br/><br/>EXAMPLE1: A vector statistical units may be represented both by an area and a point geometry. Such point can be the center of gravity or a label location.<br/><br/>EXAMPLE2: For mapping, different generalised representations of vector statistical units are required. These geometries depend on the visualisation scale.<br/><br/><br/>
