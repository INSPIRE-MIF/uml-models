-- Name --<br/>Coastline<br/><br/>-- Definition --<br/>A special case of a shoreline defined as the shoreline at Mean High Water (MHW). Where there is not significant variation in water level, Mean Sea Level (MSL) can be used as a substitute for MHW. <br/><br/>-- Description --<br/>This feature type is designed for the general use case where the land:sea boundary is required to support general discovery and viewing of datasets.  It is not designed to infer any political boundaries.  The purpose is to provide a consistent overview of European marine extent and for this reason a single tidal extent is selected.  This is consistent with IHO-57 definitions<br/>
