-- Name --<br/>environmental stratification classification value<br/><br/>-- Definition --<br/>Codes for climatic stratification of the Environment of Europe.<br/><br/>-- Description --<br/>Based on environmental variables (climate, geomorphology, oceanicity and northing) using a Principal component analysis and ISODATA clustering routine. The Environmental Stratification of Europe (EnS) consists of 84 Strata, which have been aggregated to 13 Environmental Zones with a spatial resolution of 1 km².<br/><br/>NOTE This stratification is after Metzger et al. 2005.<br/><br/>

Notes: -- Name --<br/>alpine north<br/><br/>-- Definition --<br/>The mountains and uplands of western Scandinavia together with the narrow coastal plain.<br/><br/>-- Description --<br/>The Zone has been heavily glaciated typified not only by deep U-shaped valleys but also by the indented fjord coastline. In the British uplands there are extensive plantations of spruce, but otherwise the forest cover is mostly deciduous.<br/><br/><br/>
