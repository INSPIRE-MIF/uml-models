-- Name --<br/>Site<br/>-- Definition --<br/>All land  at a same or distinct geographic location under the management control of a holding covering activities, products and services. This includes all infrastructure, equipment and materials. The geometry of the site  must be a point or a surface. Mulitpoint or multisurface is not allowed.<br/><br/>-- Description --<br/>The  site  can be represented by a point or by a surface.<br/>the site can have only one geometry.<br/><br/>
