Notes: -- Name --<br/>Universe<br/><br/>-- Definition --<br/>When distribution is related to a subset of the population and not the population in its whole, the literal description of the way this subset was defined.<br/><br/>EXAMPLE : A dataset can give the population distribution of the unemployed persons under 25.
