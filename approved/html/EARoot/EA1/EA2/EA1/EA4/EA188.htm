Planar foliation defined by a tabular succession of layers #gt; 5 mm thick. This definition is based on the proposed definition of gneiss by the NADM Science Language Technical Team, so that the GeologicStructure characteristic of gneiss is layering. The committee discussed the possibility that layering should be considered a kind of foliation, but the majority opinion was that it is a different kind of structure.<br/><br/>Kept so that instance documents have a "Layering" tag<br/>
