The PhysicalProperties Application Schema permits description of physical properties of earth materials, geologic units and geologic structures.  The model borrows from the SWECommon model to describe scalar, vector and tensor values of physical property measurements such as density, porosity, permeability, magnetic susceptibility, magnetic remanence, and other geophysical properties.
