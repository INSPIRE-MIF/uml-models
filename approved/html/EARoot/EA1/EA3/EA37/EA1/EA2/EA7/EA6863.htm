In many applications specimen preparation procedures are applied to the material prior to its use in an observation. The class <i>PreparationStep</i> (Figure 13) shall link a SF_Specimen to a OM_Process that describes a phase of the specimen preparation.  It shall support one attribute. <br/><br/>
