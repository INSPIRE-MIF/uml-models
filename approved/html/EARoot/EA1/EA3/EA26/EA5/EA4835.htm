The type "MM_Route" describes routes defined by a geometric object (a composite curve), and starts and stops positions on the geometry. MM_Route is the counterpart of NT_Route in ISO 19133. Therefore, the properties and the behaviors of MM_Route are the same except that MM_Route returns a sequence of route segments and MM_Route aggregates an ordered set of trip schemes.
