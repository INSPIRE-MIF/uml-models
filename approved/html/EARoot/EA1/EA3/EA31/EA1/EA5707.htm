The class LC_LCMLRegister specifies information about the register itself. It is a subclass of the class RE_Register which is defined in ISO 19135. It inherits 6 attributes from ISO 19135. Two of the attributes are specialized. The other attributes are taken directly from ISO 19135.<br/><br/>ISO 19135 specifies that the attribute <i>name</i> shall be a character string that is used to uniquely identify a register within the set of registers maintained by the register owner.  In the case the LCML register shall be called:<br/>"Land Cover Meta Language Register".<br/><br/>ISO 19135 specifies that the attribute<i> contentSummary</i> shall be a character string containing a general statement of the purpose for which items in the register are made, including any limits to the scope of the register, and the types of applications for which the items are intended. In the case the LCML register <i>contentSummary</i> shall be contain:<br/>"Register of LCML metaclasses used to extend the LCML metaclasses LC_ElementCharacteristic and LC_ClassCharacteristic and the extension only of LC_Element ".<br/><br/>
