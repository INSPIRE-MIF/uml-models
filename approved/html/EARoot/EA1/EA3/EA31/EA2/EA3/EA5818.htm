The LC_UnconsolidatedSedimentsSurfaceElement metaobject is a subtype of LC_NaturalSurfaceElement. This metaclass, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all unconsolidated natural surfaces. It has two subtypes LC_BareSoil and LC_LooseAndShiftingSand.<br/>
