The LC_WoodyGrowthForms metaobject is a subtype of LC_GrowthForm and has two subtypes LC_Tree and LC_Shrub and two components LC_WoodyLeafType and LC_WoodyLeafPhenology. The relationship <i>leafType</i> is to LC_WoodyLeafType and the relationship <i>leafPhenology</i> is to LC_WoofyLeafPhenology. <br /></p><p>LC_WoodyGrowthLeafType is an association class on the component relationship between the class LC_WoodyLeafType  and LC_WoodyGrowthForm. LC_WoodyGrowthLeafPhenology is an association class on the component relationship between the class LC_WoodyLeafPhenology and LC_WoodyGrowthForm.<br /></p><p>This metaobject, when instantiated, allows the generation of a general land cover feature class in an LCCS for all woody growth form vegetation covered surfaces. <br /></p>
