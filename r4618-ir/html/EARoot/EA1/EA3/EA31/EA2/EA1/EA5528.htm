The code list LC_ElementPresenceType contains a list of types of temporal relationships. This list contains four types <i>fixed, exclusive, conditional</i> and <i>sequentialTemporal.</i> This list may be extended through registration. The attribute <i>presenceType</i> for the association class LC_ElementRelationInSameStratum is optional and is by default the first value of this enumeration "<i>fixed</i>".The presence types are::<br /></p><p><br /></p><p><b><i>fixed</i></b><b> </b>(mandatory)- The LC_Element exists in the strata <br /></p><p>    <b><i>exclusive</i></b><b> </b>- One of two LC_Elements may exist in the stratum (XOR relationship)<br /></p><p>    <b><i>conditional</i></b><b> </b>(optional)- The existence of an LC_Element in a strata may exist dependant upon the existance of another LC_Element<br /></p><p>    <b><i>sequentialTemporal</i></b><b> </b>-Two LC_Elements may exist in a strata linked with a sequential temporal relationship.<br /></p><p><br /></p>
