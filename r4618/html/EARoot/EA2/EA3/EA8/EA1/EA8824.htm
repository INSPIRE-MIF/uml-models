-- Definition --<br /></p><p>Geographical areas characterised by specific ecological conditions,<br /></p><p>processes, structure and (life support) functions that physically support the organisms that live there.  <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Description --<br /></p><p>Includes terrestrial and aquatic areas distinguished by geographical, abiotic and biotic features, whether entirely natural or semi-natural.  <br /></p><p>NOTE Many other attributes may be added to this class when the theme is properly developed as part of Annex III.
