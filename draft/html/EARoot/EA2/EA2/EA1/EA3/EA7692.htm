-- Name --<br /></p><p>spot elevation<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Definition --<br /></p><p>Point spatial object which describes the elevation of an Earth's surface at a specific location. It provides a single elevation property value.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Description --<br /></p><p>NOTE 1 It often represents a singular point in the morphology of a land or water body's floor surface.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>NOTE 2 The attribute propertyType is equal to 'height' in the case of spot heights. It is equal to 'depth' in the case of spot depths.<br /></p>
