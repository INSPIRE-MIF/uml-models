-- Definition --<br /></p><p>A tract periodically covered by flood water, excluding tidal waters.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Description --<br /></p><p>SOURCE [DFDD].<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>NOTE 1 It may be caused by either uncontrolled inundation (for example: flooding due to a river overflowing its banks or low-lying regions accumulating standing water following severe rainfall) or controlled inundation (for example: flooded by the regulation of the level of a reservoir). [DFDD].<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>NOTE 2 Inundation for irrigation purposes is excluded.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>NOTE 3 Regarded as a candidate spatial object in Annex III theme 'Natural risk zones' due to the relationship (by definition) with flooding.<br /></p>
