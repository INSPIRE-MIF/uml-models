Notes: -- Definition --<br /></p><p>Anode.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>-- Description --<br /></p><p>An <i>anode </i>is a feature (specifically, an electrical mechanism) that’s applied to system components for the prevention of rust, pitting, and the corrosion of metal surfaces that are in contact with water or soil.
