Additional information may be provided to further refine metaobjects within the LCML by the use of Characteristics. A class characteristic is a component aggregated to an entire LC_LandCover metaobject. An LC_LandCover metaobject is instantiated directly to a Legend Class within a LCCS and so the ClassCharacteristic is additional information to refine an entire class. The use of characteristics in the LCML is optional.<br /></p><p>The component LC_ClassCharacteristic is related to LC_LandCover by the relation <i>characteristic</i>. It has four subtypes LC_Climate, LC_LandForm, LC_TopographicalAspects and LC_SurfaceCharacteristic.<br /></p>
